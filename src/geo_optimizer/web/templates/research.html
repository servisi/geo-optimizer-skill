Schema Markup & AI Citations

Growth Marshal, Feb 2026 — 730 pages
Rich schema (5+ attributes): 61.7% citation rate. Generic schema: 41.6%. Generic actually underperforms no schema.
How we use it: Schema richness check differentiates rich vs generic. Generic gets zero bonus.

AI Citations Report 2026

OtterlyAI — 1M+ citations analyzed
73% of sites have technical barriers blocking AI crawlers. Reference-grade content gets 3-5x more citations.
How we use it: robots.txt, CDN, and JS rendering checks address the 73% barrier problem.

AI Mode Citation Factors

SE Ranking, 2025 — 2.3M pages
Flesch-Kincaid Grade 6-8 is the sweet spot. Sections of 100-150 words are optimal. FAQ schema has zero impact — only real FAQ content matters.
How we use it: Readability, passage density, FAQ-in-content, and section length checks.
GEO Optimizer focuses on infrastructure optimization (robots.txt, llms.txt, schema, meta, content structure) — not content manipulation. The adversarial research confirms that manipulative techniques degrade the ecosystem.
